SMT-COMP 2022

The 17th International Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP 2022) is part of the SMT Workshop 2022, affiliated with IJCAR 2022. The SMT Workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the competition.

Key dates

  • Mar 15   Deadline for new benchmark contributions
  • May 28   Final versions of competition tools
  • June 18   Deadline for first versions of solvers (for all tracks)
  • July 2   Deadline for final versions of solvers, including system descriptions
  • August 12   SMT Workshop (presentation of results)

Organizers

  • Haniel Barbosa (chair) - Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais, Brazil
  • François Bobot - CEA List, France
  • Jochen Hoenicke - Albert-Ludwigs-Universität Freiburg, Germany

SMT-COMP 2022 is organized under the direction of the SMT Steering Committee.
The competition is run on StarExec, a service that is hosted at the University of Iowa. The parallel and cloud tracks are run on AWS.

SMT-COMP 2019

The 14th International Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP 2019) is part of the SMT Workshop 2019, affiliated with SAT 2019. The SMT Workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the competition.

News

  • 07 Jul 2019 SMT-COMP 2019 results available

  • 06 Jun 2019 SMT-COMP 2019 started running

  • 19 Apr 2019 SMT-COMP 2019 rules draft available

  • 22 Jan 2019 SMT-COMP 2019 call for comments, benchmarks, solvers

Key Dates

  • March 1   Deadline for new benchmark contributions
  • May 1   Final versions of competition tools
  • May 19   Deadline for first versions of solvers (for all tracks)
  • June 2   Deadline for final versions of solvers, including system descriptions
  • July 7-8   SMT Workshop (presentation of results)

Organizers

  • Liana Hadarean - Amazon, USA
  • Antti Hyvarinen - Universita della Svizzera italiana, Switzerland
  • Aina Niemetz (co-chair) - Stanford University, USA
  • Giles Reger (co-chair) - University of Manchester, UK

SMT-COMP 2019 is organized under the direction of the SMT Steering Committee.
The competition is run on StarExec, a service that is hosted at the University of Iowa.

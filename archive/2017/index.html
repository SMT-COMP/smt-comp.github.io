SMT-COMP 2017

The 12th International Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP 2017) is part of the SMT Workshop 2017, affiliated with CAV 2017. The SMT Workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the competition.

News

  • 23 Jul 2017 SMT-COMP 2017 results presented

  • 20 Jun 2017 SMT-COMP 2017 competition jobs started

  • 21 Apr 2017 SMT-COMP 2017 draft rules posted

  • 15 Mar 2017 SMT-COMP 2017 call for comments, benchmarks, solvers

Key Dates

  • May 1   Deadline for new benchmark contributions
  • June 4   Deadline for first versions of solvers
  • June 19   Deadline for final versions of solvers
  • July 22-23   SMT Workshop (presentation of results)

Organizers

  • Matthias Heizmann - Universität Freiburg, Germany
  • Giles Reger - University of Manchester, UK
  • Tjark Weber (chair) - Uppsala University, Sweden

SMT-COMP 2017 is organized under the direction of the SMT Steering Committee.
The competition is run on StarExec, a service that is hosted at the University of Iowa.

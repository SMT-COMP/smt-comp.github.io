SMT-COMP 2015

The 10th International Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP 2015) is part of the SMT Workshop 2015, associated with CAV 2015. The SMT Workshop and the main CAV conference will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the competition.

News

  • 8 Feb 2015 Call for Comments issued
  • 11 May 2015 Revised rules posted
  • 15 June 2015 Competition jobs started
  • 19 July 2015 Results presented

Key Dates

  • May 1 2015   Deadline for new benchmark contributions
  • June 1 2015   Deadline for first versions of solvers
  • June 14 2015   Deadline for final versions of solvers
  • July 18-19 2015   SMT Workshop (presentation of results)

Organizers

  • Sylvain Conchon - Paris-Sud University, France
  • David Déharbe - Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte, Brazil
  • Tjark Weber - Uppsala University, Sweden (chair)

The competition uses the StarExec service, which is hosted at the University of Iowa.

SMT-COMP 2023

The 18th International Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP 2023) is part of the SMT Workshop 2023, affiliated with CADE-29. The SMT Workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the competition.

Key dates

  • Mar 31   Deadline for new benchmark contributions
  • April 29   Final versions of competition tools
  • May 13   Deadline for first versions of solvers (for all tracks)
  • May 27   Deadline for final versions of solvers, including system descriptions
  • July 5–6   SMT Workshop (presentation of results)

Organizers

  • François Bobot (chair) - CEA List, France
  • Martin Bromberger - MPI für Informatik, Germany
  • Jochen Hoenicke - Certora, Israel

SMT-COMP 2023 is organized under the direction of the SMT Steering Committee.
The competition is run on StarExec, a service that is hosted at the University of Iowa. The parallel and cloud tracks are run on AWS.

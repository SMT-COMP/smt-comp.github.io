SMT-COMP 2014

The 2014 edition of the Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP) (July 17-18, 2014) is part of the SMT 2014 workshop, associated with SAT 2014 at the Vienna Summer of Logic (July 9-24, 2014, Vienna, AU). In particular, SMT-COMP is part of the FLoC'14 Olympic Games. The SMT workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the SMTCOMP competition. There will also be an awards ceremony on July 21, as part of the Olympic Games.

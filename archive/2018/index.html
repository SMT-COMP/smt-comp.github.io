SMT-COMP 2018

The 13th International Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP 2018) is part of the SMT Workshop 2018, affiliated with FLoC 2018. The SMT Workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the competition.

Key Dates

  • April 8   Deadline for new benchmark contributions
  • May 19   Deadline for first versions of solvers
  • June 10   Deadline for final versions of solvers
  • July 12-13   SMT Workshop (presentation of results)

Organizers

  • Matthias Heizmann - Universität Freiburg, Germany
  • Aina Niemetz - Stanford University, USA
  • Giles Reger - University of Manchester, UK
  • Tjark Weber (chair) - Uppsala University, Sweden

SMT-COMP 2018 is organized under the direction of the SMT Steering Committee.
The competition is run on StarExec, a service that is hosted at the University of Iowa.

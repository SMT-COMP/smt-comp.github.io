SMT-COMP 2020

The 15th International Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP 2020) is part of the SMT Workshop 2020, affiliated with IJCAR 2020. The SMT Workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the competition.

Organizers

  • Haniel Barbosa - Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais, Brazil
  • Jochen Hoenicke - Albert-Ludwigs-Universität Freiburg, Germany
  • Antti Hyvarinen (chair) - Universita della Svizzera italiana, Switzerland

SMT-COMP 2020 is organized under the direction of the SMT Steering Committee.
The competition is run on StarExec, a service that is hosted at the University of Iowa.
